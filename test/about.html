ABOUT US

We are all about the mission to revolutionize the beauty industry to be equal and diverse. We work hard towards empowering female initiatives within a multitude of communities.

Not Sorry Cosmetics is an idea of empowering the female through cosmetics and having a chance to evolve and revolutionize today's beauty. With acknowledging the fight on cruel cosmetic animal testing, and to diversify the western beauty standard which would open it's doors to every type of women, no matter what their background and ethnicity. Making a product for the individual rather than the imposed societal idea of a woman.
